Lecture Notes Sharing Page



Let's Lecture is a publishing platform for scientific research. A professional network that allows scientists and researchers to communicate and share information. If you have met the requirements to create a Let's Lecture account, you can now add your own research as an article. English is used globally on the site, but you can also share content in your own language if you wish.





On this page, the notes of the related courses are shared. Courses and related documents of various engineering departments of universities can be found on this page. As Let's Lecture, we are happy to assist all students who are members with their course documents. Students or logged-in members can access the preliminary information about the course they want and the lecture notes of those courses free of charge. Thanks to Let's Lecture, they will both feel comfortable about their lecture notes and will be better equipped for their exams. Then don't be late, dear students, LET'S LECTURE!!!
